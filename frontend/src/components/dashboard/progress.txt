## ⭐ Development Priority (the roadmap I'd follow)

1. **Study Workspace** (make it genuinely useful)
2. **Progress Overview**
3. **Roadmap Widget**
4. **Quick Actions**
5. **Upcoming Schedule**
6. **AI Coach**
7. **Weekly Progress**
8. **Study Streak**
9. **Achievements**
10. **Final polish** (animations, responsive design, theming, notifications)

This order keeps your dashboard useful at every stage instead of spending time polishing features before the core study workflow is complete. It also aligns well with your long-term vision of making the roadmap and AI coach the heart of the application.
